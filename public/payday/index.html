Introduction to Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish known for its delicious blend of rice, tomatoes, and spices...

Ingredients for Jollof Rice

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 can (400g) of chopped tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3-4 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger, grated or minced
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper or hot pepper (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions for Making Jollof Rice

  1. If using chicken or another protein, season it with salt, pepper, and your choice of spices...
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent.
  3. Add the diced red and green bell peppers, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir for a few minutes until the peppers soften.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce or chopped tomatoes and cook for about 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Wash the rice thoroughly to remove excess starch. Drain the water and add the rice to the pot. Stir to ensure the rice is well coated with the tomato mixture.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and allow the rice to cook for about 20-25 minutes. Avoid stirring the rice during this time to prevent it from becoming mushy.
  7. Once the rice is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
